About GovTrack

GovTrack is a unified digital governance platform that empowers transparency and efficiency in public spending. It streamlines budget allocation, milestone tracking, and public accountability through a role-based real-time dashboard.

Key Features

  • ✅ Real-time fund tracking and allocation
  • ✅ Geo-tagged and phase-wise project updates
  • ✅ Role-based secure access control
  • ✅ Transparent dashboards for the public
  • ✅ Auto-generated reports for audits and reviews
  • ✅ Smart notifications and deadline alerts

Use Cases

  • 🛣️ Monitor infrastructure projects (roads, bridges, public works)
  • 🏥 Track healthcare funding and hospital modernization
  • 🏫 Manage educational grants and school construction
  • 💡 Improve utility services (water, electricity, sanitation)

How It Works

  1. 1️⃣ Admins allocate funds via a secure portal
  2. 2️⃣ Officials update progress across phases
  3. 3️⃣ Ministers oversee and approve strategic reports
  4. 4️⃣ Public accesses updates via transparent dashboards
